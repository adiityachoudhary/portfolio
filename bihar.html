About Bihar

Bihar, a state in eastern India, is renowned for its rich history, cultural heritage, and significant landmarks such as Bodh Gaya and Nalanda University, attracting tourists worldwide.
Bodh Gaya

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the place where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree.
Rajgir

A scenic town with historical significance, featuring sites like Griddhakuta Hill, Vishwa Shanti Stupa, and the ruins of ancient Rajgir Fort.

Takht Sri Harimandir Sahib

Takht Sri Harimandir Sahib, or Patna Sahib, is a revered Sikh shrine in Bihar, India, honoring the birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh Ji, the tenth Sikh Guru.
